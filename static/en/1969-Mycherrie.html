Second stage: Young Stevie Wonder

MY CHERIE AMOUR

1969

With My Cherie Amour great songs that managed to enter the top ten U.S. as "My Cherie Amour" -recorded 3 years earlier "Yester-Me, Yester-You, Yesterday", and some very special versions of "Light My Fire" and "Hello, Young Lovers", with a sound very similar to the success achieved with "For Once In My Life". Versions of "The Shadows Of Your Smile", co-written by Stevie was versioned by great artists such as Eva Cassidy, Lou Rawls or Tony Bennett.

It will be the final years of collaboration in the production of Henry Cosby, and the beginning of a greater involvement of Stevie Wonder in all sections construction of the discs.
