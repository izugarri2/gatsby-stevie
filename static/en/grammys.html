GRAMMYS

The Grammys are the most important prizes to which any musician can aspire. Since 1959 the National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences of the United States grant this prizes to recognize and respect the merits that have been achieved in the world of music. Stevie Wonder has the honor of being one of the most successful artists with a total of 25 Grammy awards in various categories, highlighting the decade of the 70's where he monopolized a large number of major awards.
