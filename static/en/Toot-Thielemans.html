Two geniuses on harmonica;
Toots Thielemans & Stevie Wonder

Throughout the extensive musical career Stevie Wonder, there have been many collaborations he has done for other artists. Artists that in the vast majority of cases have been friends with whom he has cast feelings and admiration. His extraordinary generosity and multifaceted ability has allowed feel all aspects of music.
Occasions full of mysticism and charm as that could live in the ceremony of the "Polar Music Prize Award" in Stockholm (Sweden) in the year 1999. There stood on stage the Belgian Toots Thielemans, one of the greatest interpreters of history with the harmonica, that can be found even in such famous movies as "Breakfast at Tiffany's", "Midnight Cowboy" or "Bagdad Café".

While playing one of his "jazz standards" -Bluesette-, Stevie Wonder sitting listened with the public and could not resist the temptation to do a honor to a master harmonica, suddenly appearing on stage to share with him one of the moments magical and unforgettable from the music.
Complicity, emotion, love, friendship ... everything a sonorous and visual luxury to be able to enjoy two geniuses live giving free rein to the imagination that springs from this instrument small in size, but huge when it comes to transmitting emotions.
