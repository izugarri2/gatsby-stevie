Compilation

ESSENTIAL

1987

Continuing the commercial pull of the latest albums of Stevie, Motown publishes a double compilation album mastered at Abbey Road Studios in London with songs between 1962 and 1971 style jazz-soul-beat, revealing the new public of the material of those years. His name Essential.
