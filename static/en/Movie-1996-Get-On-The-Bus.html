GET ON THE BUS: Redemption Song

1996

Once again we have to stevie participating with a song for a film directed by Spike Lee. It's Get On The Bus filmed in 1996. The film tells the bus trip was organized in the historical concentration brought hundreds of thousands of African Americans in the U.S. capital in order to claim for the rights of the black minority. During the long journey will be highlighted their feelings, ideologies, claims ...

Stevie Wonder is into the soundtrack with "Redemption Song". A classic by Bob Marley and a hymn of liberation and against the bondage of the bodies and souls.
