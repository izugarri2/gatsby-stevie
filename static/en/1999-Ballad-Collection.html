BALLAD COLLECTION

1999


Ballad Collection is a compilation over Stevie Wonder, but this more focused in the ballads and for his audience in Britain. Stevie Wonder is credited to possess exquisite sensitivity and good taste in their compositions, which added to his skill in interpreting results in an album for the more romantics.
