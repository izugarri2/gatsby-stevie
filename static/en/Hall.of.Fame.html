25th Anniversary
Rock & Roll Hall Of Fame

Child prodigy since began at Motown, an icon of music, a spirit with soul and a pioneer of the music whose influence has spread from reggae and jazz, to soul and pop music. He reflected as anyone in the 70's avant-garde synth sound with soul, funky rhythms and melodies with their own light that illuminated the musical path into the future.
Talk about Stevie Wonder would have no end, are almost 50 years full of rich compositions and fantastic interpretations. Are also countless awards and recognitions he has received throughout his life, remember that began with only 11 years old, and in point of recognition can include awards Grammy, an Oscar, Gershwin Prize..., and also honored to be included in the Rock 'N Roll Hall of Fame since 1989.

In this sites honors the most famous and influential artists of the music taking you meet certain requirements to be part of that museum, having spent more than 25 years after releasing their first album and be chosen by a jury of academics, producers and journalists who vote for those artists who think they have had a clear and obvious influence in the history of music. This way we find artists as Marvin Gaye, Otis Redding, The Rolling Stones, or Van Morrison.
In the year 2009 was celebrated the 25th anniversary of his founding with a concert in a big way on 29 and 30 October at Madison Square Garden, there will be artists as Bruce Springsteen & The E Street Band, Simon & Garfunkel, Paul Simon, Crosby Still Nash & Friends, U2, Aretha Franklin, Eric Clapton, Metallica and Stevie Wonder. A whole new opportunity to enjoy good music in the company of some of the greatest artists who has music.
