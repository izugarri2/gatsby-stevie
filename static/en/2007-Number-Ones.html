Compilation

NUMBER ONES

2007


With Number Ones the Motown provides an overview of some of the Stevie Wonder's hit singles from Stevie Wonder since his inception in the early 60's to A Time To Love.

A small sample of the talent and variety of songs consecrated of Stevie. The number of songs strikes me as weak and difficult to frame in one cd. The successes of Stevie in his 50 years of music are better contained in other collections, but if you not know a lot of Stevie and want a tasty snack ...
