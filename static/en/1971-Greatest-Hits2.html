Compilation

GREATEST HITS VOL.2

1971

Second compilation bringing together singles from 1968 to 1971. It is a good collection of songs showing the compositional and interpretive talents of Stevie Wonder.

Sounds of the 60's and his latest album "Where I'm Coming From" with sounds more sophisticated, displaying incredible sounds easy to handle soul, pop/rock and ballads.
