STEVIE WONDER, A MUSICAL COLOSSUS

Concerts, live shows, live performances
     from the 60's to this new millennium


Giant, genius, messiah, titan..., Stevie Wonder is a musical colossus that has stood out since he barely had use of reason. He is a man consecrated by and for music, as well as a being enlightened by the divine. The music that flows through his head shocks you, and his voice unique shakes you, managing to convey emotions in a natural way. Awarded and decorated by their own merits, there are numerous the awards he has received along a rich and extensive musical career that already covers a whopping six decades. In all of them we can find the musical imprint of this man who will last forever in our lives.

From the delightful melodies of "My Cherie Amour" or "I Was Made To Love Her" from the 60s, to the glorious stage of the 70s with "Sir Duke", "Superstition" or "You Are The Sunshine Of My Life", without forgetting that in the 80's and 90's he also scored songs like "Master blaster", "I Just Call To Say I Love You" or "For Your Love". The new millennium -although something soulless in the creative, since we can only count on a notable album titled A Time To Love- takes over with great tours and live review of his musical legacy.

A man who has known to remain decade after decade true to himself and to others, claiming some basic pillars of life as the love, the freedom, the peace, the beauty and the passion. An example of this are his concerts and shows that he has been developing to long and wide of the whole planet for all these decades, always surrounded by good musicians and voices.
