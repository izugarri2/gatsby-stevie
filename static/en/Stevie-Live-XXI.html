STEVIE WONDER LIVE

Concerts, live shows, live performances in the new millennium


The new millennium is marked by the inexplicable death of Stevie's mother -Mama Lula- in the year 2006. It is the trigger for Stevie a year later to make his first tour in 10 years. It was something what his mother wanted him to do, and that gave him the strength to start a tour of the US and Canada in 2008, continuing through Europe and Australia, giving birth to his first official DVD entitled Live At Last recorded in London the year 2008.

Concerts and tours that have not stopped in recent years surrounded as always by an excellent band of musicians, touring major world events such as Rock In Rio 2011 and 2012, the 25th anniversary of Rock And Roll Of Fame, or festivals such as Glastonbury and Calling in United Kingdom, or the Global Citizen of New York. A man who, despite the passage of decades, is associated with the passion and emotion necessary to be considered the greatest musician of the music of our time
