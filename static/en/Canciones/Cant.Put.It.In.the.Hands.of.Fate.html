Can't Put It In the Hands of Fate


Composer * Stevie Wonder
Produced and arranged * Stevie Wonder
Musicians * Nathan Watts: bass; Stanley Randolph: Drums; Munyungo Jackson: percussion; Will Wheaton, Traci Nelson, Phylicia Hill, Kimberly Brewer, George Young, Cory Rooney, Camille Grigsby & Allison Semmes: background vocals; Stevie Wonder: harmonica, keyboards, choir Arrangement
Collaborations * Busta Rhymes, Rapsody, Cordae and Chika

Video

"Can't Put It In the Hands of Fate" is one of the two songs that Stevie Wonder has advanced in October 2020 as part of an EP or a complete album on which Stevie has been working for a long time, Through The Eyes Of Wonder. This is a funky, rhythmic song featuring the collaboration of rappers Busta Rhymes, Rapsody, Cordae and Chika. He connects with the anti-racist demands of the Black Lives Matter movement, and emphasizes the need and primes to continue with the mobilizations for equality with expressions such as "You say you're sick and tired of us protesting and I say Not time enough to make a change; You say 'Just keep holdin' on', I say 'No, way, 'cause we can't put it in the hands of fate'".
