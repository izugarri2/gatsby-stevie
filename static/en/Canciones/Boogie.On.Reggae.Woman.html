Boogie On Reggae Woman


1974 album FULFILLINGNESS' FIRST FINALE


Composer * Stevie Wonder
Produced & Arranged * Stevie Wonder
Musicians * Rocky: Congas, Stevie Wonder: all instruments

Video

With "Boogie On Reggae Woman" reached to number 1 in the charts, and receives the Grammy for Best performance male R&B. The song is, despite the title, style funk / R&B, playing with the rhythms and harmonica, expressing their tastes for women and excellent use of the Moog synthesizer bassline and harmonica.
