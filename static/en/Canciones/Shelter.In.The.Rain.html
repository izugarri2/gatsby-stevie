Shelter In The Rain


2005 album A TIME TO LOVE


Composer * Stevie Wonder
Produced & Arranged * Stevie Wonder
Musicians * Narada Michael Walden: Drums, Stevie Wonder: all other instruments, T.Atkins, T.Burrell, J.Carter, D.Johnson, E.King, B.Sledge: Choir, Choir Vocals produced & arranged by: Kirk Franklin, String Arrangements: Paul Riser

Video

The planet maintains his pulse with difficulty despite this whirlwind of events with which it seems to be being punished in recent years. Uncontrolled fires devastating nature, tsunamis and earthquakes spreading tragedies, violence and selfishness of the human being sowing pain, or pandemics of invisible enemies like the one we currently suffer.

Now more than ever we need that time to love that Stevie Wonder asked for a few years ago, and to feel protected under a shelter in the rain.
