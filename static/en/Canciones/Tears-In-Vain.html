Tears In Vain



Composer * Clarence Paul
Produced & Arranged * Clarence Paul
Musicians * Funk Brothers and Stevie Wonder

* The song was -in November 1964- originally part of the B-side of a rare version of "Pretty Little Angel". In March 1965 was once again on the B side of another single, "Kiss Me Baby". In 2006 the song was included in the album: The Complete Motown Singles, Vol. 4...
