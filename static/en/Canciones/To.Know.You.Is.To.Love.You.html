To Know You Is To Love You


Composer * Stevie Wonder, Syreeta Wright

Video

In live with Stevie Wonder
Original album Syreeta
"To Know You Is To Love You" was composed by Stevie Wonder with Syreeta Wright in the early 1970s and was part of Syreeta's debut album in 1972. Receptionist, secretary, singer, and writer of Motown, whom Stevie married in 1970. Stevie Wonder produced the disc, co-wrote, and participated in the instrumentation and vocals of the album.

B.B. King versioned this song in 1973, using it to give title to his album published by ABC Records. And years later the Motown introduced the song in an album under the name of Motown Funk 2013.
