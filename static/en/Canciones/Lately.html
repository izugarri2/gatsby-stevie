LATELY


1980 album HOTTER THAN JULY


Composer * Stevie Wonder
Produced & Arranged * Stevie Wonder
Musicians * Stevie Wonder: all instruments

Video

One of those special songs that make you shudder just hearing the opening bars is Lately. A ballad that describes how overwhelming suspicion and uncertainty of a relationship in first person.

In the song was reflected the excellence by nature of a musician capable of tear your soul with his voice, able to soak up emotions with his letter, make you partaker of his doubts and sadness. Create an atmosphere of intimacy that allows feelings to come in its purest form. For this only need the company of his piano, and his extraordinary voice masterfully playing one of the most wonderful songs that have been composed.
