He's Misstra Know-It-All


1973 album INNERVISIONS


Composer * Stevie Wonder
Produced & Arranged * Stevie Wonder
Musicians * Willie Weeks: Bass, Stevie Wonder: all instruments

Video

"He's Misstra Know-It-All" was aimed at Tricky Dick, alias Richard Nixon, denounced corruption in the biggest political scandal of the century. The song has shaped soft ballad with a steady beat, with the mastery of vocal harmonies Stevie as throughout the album playing most of the instruments. Ethereal sounds of flutes provided by his TONTO modular synthesizer. Willie Weeks, on electric bass, is the only other musician. Towards the end of the song the mood changes to a stronger sense, with a voice of Stevie louder and hand-clapping highlighting the pace, half-beat and fourth-beat.

The song was released again in 1977 in the UK and the US, as the B side of "Sir Duke"
