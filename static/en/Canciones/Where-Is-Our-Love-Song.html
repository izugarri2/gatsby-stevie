Where Is Our Love Song


Composer * Stevie Wonder
Produced and arranged * Stevie Wonder
Musicians * Gary Clark Jr.: Guitar; Zuri Morris, Zaiah Morris, Warryn Campbell, Shanice Knox, Nyah Morris, Mumtaz Morris, Mandla Morris, Lamar Mitchell, Krista Campbell, Keith John, Erica Campbell & Aisha Morris: Background Vocals; Stevie Wonder: keyboards, piano, bass, harmonica, choir arrangement

Video

"Where Is Our Love Song" is one of the two songs that Stevie Wonder has advanced in October 2020 as part of an EP or a complete album on which Stevie has been working for a long time, Through The Eyes Of Wonder. This new song is a ballad where the collaboration of the guitarist winner of several Grammy Awards Gary Clark Jr. stands out, and whose proceeds will go to Feeding America in his fight against poverty. Relates that "started writing it when he was 18 years old, and suddenly this year came "all the confusion, all the hate, the east versus west, left versus right: a heartbreak … a lot of confusion". What at first was going to be a love song between a couple, has become a love song for humanity as Stevie asks in the song "Where is our song of love?, Another love song between you and me, But a song of love for all humanity, Where is our love song?, Our desperately needed song of love".
