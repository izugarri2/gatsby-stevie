Happy Birthday


1980 album HOTTER THAN JULY


Composer * Stevie Wonder
Produced & Arranged * Stevie Wonder
Musicians * Stevie Wonder: all instruments

Video

The song "Happy Birthday", was composed by Stevie Wonder in honor of Martin Luther King, assassinated a few years before. It is a statement of commitment to Stevie for civil rights while serving as support for the campaign "King's Birthday" and convert the 15th of January at national holiday in the United States.
