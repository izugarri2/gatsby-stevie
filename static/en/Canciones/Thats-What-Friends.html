That's What Friends Are For


Composers * Burt Bacharach, Carole Bayer Sager

Video

"That's What Friends Are For" is a song interpreted by Dionne Warwick and her friends Gladys Knight, Elton John and Stevie Wonder in 1985 in order to raise funds to help fight the S.I.D.A. It peaked at number 1 for 4 weeks on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. They won a Grammy for Best Duo or Vocal Group, and their songwriters Burt Bacharach, Carole Bayer Sager were also rewarded for another Grammy for Song of the Year.
