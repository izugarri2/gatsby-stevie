I Love You More


Composer* Stevie Wonder
Produced & Arranged* Stevie Wonder & Narada Michael Walden
Musicians* Narada Michael Walden: Drums, Finger Cymbals & Sleigh Bells; Geg Philllinganes:Synthesizers; Michael Thompson: Percussions; Rodney Alejandro: Wind Chimes & Seasonal Sound Effects; Stevie Wonder: armónica; Voces: Kimberly Brewer & Stevie Wonder

Video

Published on October 30, 2001 in a compilation entitled "A Very Special Christmas 5", some of the tracks were recorded in live, December 2000 in Washington, D.C. It was the fifth edition of a series of famous volumes dedicated to Christmas that brought together several of the most important artists of this new millennium, and whose benefits were in aid of the Special Olympic Games. There you could find artists as Macy Gray, Sheryl Crow, Jon Bon Jovi or Tom Petty & the Heartbreakers among others.

Stevie Wonder also appeared in another song under the title of "Merry Christmas, Baby" along with Wyclef Jean, and in this song "I Love You More" along with Kimberly Brewer.
