Part-time Lover


1985 album IN SQUARE CIRCLE


Composer * Stevie Wonder
Produced & Arranged * Stevie Wonder
Músicos * Principal vocals: Luther Vandross & Stevie Wonder, Background vocals: Syreeta Wright, Philip Bailey, Keith John, Melody McCully, Billy Durham, Peter Byrne, Renee Hardaway, Darryl Phinnessee, All instruments: Stevie Wonder

Video

Part-time Lover becomes a Stevie Wonder hit, being the first song in history to reach the number 1 of four different Billboard charts; pop, r&b, dance, and adult contemporany, also managing to be nominated for the Grammy Awards. In the song we can find the elegant voice of Luther Vandross, the sweet of his ex-wife Syreeta Wright, or the unmistakable Philip Bailey of the Earth, Wind & Fire.
