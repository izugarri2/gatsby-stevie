Keep Our Love Alive



Composer * Stevie Wonder
Produced & Arranged * Stevie Wonder
Musicians * Stevie Wonder: all instruments

Video

"Keep Our Love Alive" is the Stevie Wonder's song published in 1990 to sensitize public opinion against the inhuman representing Apartheid in South Africa, and Stevie played in this tribute to a man who fought for ideals that would lead to equality and freedom of all human beings.

Just as when one of those great living beings disappears that has given much of his life dedicating it selflessly to others, Just is thank you knowing that your spirit will always remain with us keep that love alive.
