Until You Come Back To Me (That's What I'm Gonna Do)


1977 album Looking Back-Anthology


Composer * Stevie Wonder, Clarence Paul, Morris Broadnax
Produced & Arranged * Clarence Paul
Musicians * Funk Brothers and Stevie Wonder

Video

Many are the songs of Stevie Wonder overwhelmed you with their lyrics, melody or dramatic expression of feelings. These are songs without trying to be greedy, manage to catch you for his magic and simplicity. Stevie Wonder for that is unique; emotions that surface by a mere change of intonation, a melody that will sweeten the spirit and the heart, and lyrics that you get knocked down the soul. "Until You Come Back To (That's What I'm Gonna Do)" belongs to this group of songs filled with the magic of genius, that well interpreted by him, or by other artists, reach a huge climax.

The song talk about of heartbreak and perseverance to regain the beloved. It was originally recorded by Stevie Wonder in 1967 but not published until 1977 in an Anthology of hits also called Looking Back, which was a review of his first stage musical in Motown. But it was a few years before, in 1973, when Stevie put the song in the great voice of Aretha Franklin -the Queen of Soul- so that popularized in the album Let Me In Your Life. Had a million-selling, reached number 1 on the R&B chart and number 3 on the Hot 100 chart of the United States.

A part of the great Aretha Franklin, other artists as Luther Vandross, Miki Howard, Bobby Caldwell, Hil St. Soul or Cyndi Lauper have managed to discover the natural magic and tangible that emerges every note of this song. A delightful song that transcends decades and drenches us with his spell and charm. In the video we can enjoy the presence of these two stars in a special Lady of Soul show in the year 2005. A magical and unrepeatable moment after the sad disappearance of the great diva Aretha Franklin, we'll miss you very much...
