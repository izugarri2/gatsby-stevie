Stevie is still Wonder

A while ago I read an article that "beleaguered" the existence of the last decades of music from Stevie Wonder, I am surprised at the antagonistic attitude in a later article about the Beatles, lifting it almost to the status of gods, is that true circumstances prevented us from the obvious what would have been able to determine its future in the world of music, but as an example, we have Paul McCartney, who along with John Lennon were the mainstays of the group, and since then little can be said for his creativity and existence in recent decades.
Stevie Wonder is no doubt that creative explosion was in the seventies, with songs difficult to overcome, not only for himself but for any of the artists that today we are trying to overshadow the multinationals. It is clear that the music has evolved and will continue to do so during the next years or decades, but if there is something that define and will define a true artist is his ability to make us vibrate with his lyrics, with his notes, with his voice...
In this way, Stevie Wonder, nearly fifty years after his first public appearance in the song "Fingertips", can show us again that he can excite and thrill the audience for nearly three hours of concert, show his talent and generosity, little may be required to who give all!, and if that is a lot, the words are not necessary.

This way Stevie thanked his fans to follow there, giving part of his life in his performances, sharing with us their sorrows as the death of his mother, his joys and the births of their children, their rage and pain at the injustices of everyday life. I have not the slightest doubt the veracity of a man who, despite the many successes and praise the day that is overwhelmed, it has lost the north and remains the same simple and close to that in the beginning surprised all around him at all, and many, who have shared with him at some point in their life in a recording studio, in a scene in a song or simply an "be prepared" to help you.
The future is near and you can almost touch it, their projects in the studio with an album dedicated to his mother (Gospel Inspired by Lula) to pass away in 2006, as well as other work (Through The Eyes Of Wonder) which seek to express with his music as "see" the world around us for a person born blind as he is.

Yes, if there was a time when Stevie was wonder, but that time has never passed, the ticking of the clock was stopped and the magic has made possible remain Stevie Wonder is still wonder.
