Second stage: Young Stevie Wonder

WITH A SONG IN MY HEART

1963


With A Song In My Heart, fourth album released by Stevie Wonder. The record company,the Motown, had absolute control over all his artists, limiting the power of these creative. Wanted to give a new direction to the songs of Stevie, more remote to Ray Charles with other compositions, but by no means a barrier to the outside leaving shines talented from inside this little star.

In the production Clarence Paul & M. Stephenson from the Motown team of composers.
