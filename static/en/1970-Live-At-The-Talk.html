Second stage: Young Stevie Wonder

LIVE AT THE TALK OF THE TOWN

1970

Live At The Tak is a live recording of a young Stevie Wonder just turned 21 years old and taking creative control of his work for Motown. This is a recording made in the UK with the production of Norman Smith, based on his hits of the last part of the 60's, incorporating a consistent version of "Bridge Over Troubled Water" by Simon & Garfunkel and a first listen of "Signed, Sealed, Delivered (I'm Yours)", all a classic Stevie Wonder before embarking on a studio album.
