Watch Rock In Rio 2011

Just a year ago Stevie Wonder got thrill over 100,000 spectators who packed the stage of the Rock In Rio held on American soil, being considered by the Brazilian press as the best show of the festival. For the Rock In Rio festival being held this year in European lands, Stevie spearheaded the poster of the day on June 2nd in the Parque da Bela Vista in Lisbon.

There was a review of more than 2 hours at a number of hits and songs product of a musical career started 50 years ago, and are part of the best music of the last century.

Surrounded by excellent quality musicians -with the experience of Stevie's tours in recent years- Stevie Wonder shone with blinding star power, featuring once again the magic of an unforgettable night. On the stage of Rock In Rio Lisbon, was paralyzed for a few hours the time, and a mist came over emotionally charged of all present, resonating a devoted audience that fell exhausted before a man destined to move and make us happy with his music, showing that he is and remains unique and great.
