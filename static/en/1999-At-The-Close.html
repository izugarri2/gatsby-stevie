AT THE CLOSE OF A CENTURY

1999

He is one of the composers and performers who have had more influence to the music of the twentieth century, and what better proof that this legacy as a compilation of four CDs, including in the 70 songs, a lifetime to put into notes, words and rhythms, the key of the life; feelings, emotions, moods and energy, high energy music.

At The Clode Of A Century consists of four CDs containing 70 songs in chronological order of publication, therefore certified Gold in the United States of America.
