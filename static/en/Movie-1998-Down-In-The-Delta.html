DOWN IN THE DELTA: If Ever

1998
Down In The Delta is a drama which means the debut directing behind the camera of the sadly defunct and muse of freedom author African-American Maya Angelou. The film tells the story of a woman on the verge of self-destruction in a dangerous neighborhood of Chicago, where drug trafficking and violence are the day to day.

Stevie Wonder participates with a song he wrote with Stephanie Andrews in 1985 to John Denver and rescues in the year 1998 for the soundtrack of this film. A beautiful song entitled "If Ever".
