Second stage: Young Stevie Wonder

STEVIE WONDER LIVE

1970


Live album recorded in USA with interpretation of some of his hits, incorporating other compositions, giving them a personal touch. Fantastic domain of the harmonica -how we can hear listening "Alfie"- brilliant in the way as he use their voice, and very suprise his easy merging big band sounds with more funky.

It is a transitional shot between the sound of the 60 and with which pleasantly surprised us in the 70's.
