Stevie in Europe

No one doubts the extraordinary state musical in live of Stevie Wonder. They are more than a decade that leads through all the continents, proving a brutal vitality and a majestic musical set by excellent musicians able to leave satisfied the public most demanding.
A few years ago back to Europe to participate in a number of concerts and festivals; Calling Festival (UK), Esch-Sur-Alzette (Luxemburg), Kongsberg Jazz Festival (Norway), Roskilde Festival (Denmark), Helsinki Classic Festival (Finland), North Sea Jazz Festival(Netherlands), Vienne Jazz Festival and Juan Les Pins - Antibes Jazz Festival (France), Montreux Jazz Festival (Switzerland), and Lucca Summer Festival (Italy) are examples of such "second youth" from the most authentic and influential living artist of the black music our time. In these concerts again surrendered to his loyal audience throughout his life enjoyed and thrilled with many of his songs.

Again this year 2016 Stevie returns to Europe, particularly in London this July 10 at the summer festival BST in the central Hyde Park. Will perform in its entirety one of the best albums in the history of music in his 40th years of publication. SONGS IN THE KEY OF LIFE.

The music has been gently stroking Stevie Wonder along all his life... and Stevie has matched generosity in a catharsis of perfect assembly. A combination as natural and necessary as hydrogen and oxygen leading water, and this latter for the existence of life.
